About Immaculate

Immaculate began her career as an artist, specializing in sculpture with
a bachelor's degree in fine art. She taught as a sculptor at a senior high school
for eleven years.

course in graphic design and is now venturing into web development at Redi
School of Digital Integratioin. Outside of work, she enjoys spending time with
family and designing products as a hobby. She is skilled in Adobe Photoshop
and Illustrator. She is profecient in understanding, speaking, writing and reading
in english.
